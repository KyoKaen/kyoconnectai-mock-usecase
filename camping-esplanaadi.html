Tuesday, 14 May 2019

This is a car free campsite. The campsite is perfectly located between the city centre and the beach.  

https://www.google.ee/maps/place/Camping+Esplanaadi/@58.3809099,24.4952129,17z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m5!3m4!1s0x46ecfd1761f21df5:0x101a7641b2dcc212!8m2!3d58.3809099!4d24.4974016?hl=et

The wonderful PÃ¤rnu beach is only 5 minute walk. The city centre is also 5 minute walk but just in the opposite direction. Everything is within the walking distance: beach, city centre, supermarkets, shopping centres, bus station, banks, cinema, museums, spas, cafes, restaurants, bars, night clubs, bicycle rent etc. 

The site is quite small and it is in the yard of a private house. There are  2 toilets, 2 showers with hot water and a kitchenette equipped with a fridge, gas cooker, electric kettle and a microwave as well as a sink.

Parking in the neighbourhood is fee paying. I don´t provide parking places.

Check in - after 3pm
Check out -before 11 am
There is no reception. Just get in, pitch your tent and feel yourself at home. The campsite owner will come up to you  and then you will pay. Only cash is accepted.
             Perfect for cyclists/motocyclists, backpackers and hitch hikers
