    CAMPING DE LYON

    Camping de Lyon

    LOCATED IN A BEAUTIFUL NATURAL PARK, AT THE GATEWAY TO LYON

    Open all the year

    Camping de Lyon**** welcomes campers in a natural 6 hectare park, ideally located for visiting Lyon and the Beaujolais…
    Quality services for you to relax after a busy day out in the city.
    Come back to the green setting of the campsite and make the most of the quality facilities: a bright, modern central lodge opening onto a lovely terrace overlooking the site, a children’s playground, aheated swimming pool to cool off on a sunny day…

    Choose a nature stopover close to the city

    Set up your caravan, motorhome, or tent on a lovely pitch or opt for rental accommodation with all the comforts: wood trailers, mobile homes, or Wood & Canvas tents, the choice is yours! An ideal site for a stopover on your way to your holiday destination or a city break to explore the beautiful city of Lyon, classified World Heritage by UNESCO!

    HOW TO GET THERE?

    By train: TGV station – Lyon Perrache (11 km), Lyon Part-Dieu (15 km), Lyon Vaise (9 km)
    By plane: Lyon Saint Exupéry Airport (44 km)

    Access via the A6 motorway, exit Limonest/Dardilly (coming from the South: exit 33.2 / coming from the North: exit 33)
    Public transport at the entrance of the campsite
    Easy access to the Lyon suburbs: 25 min. from Bron and 30 min. from Givors
