Business Plan Version 0.0

KyoConnectAI Startup:

Executive Summary

Section: 5. Operations & Execution

1

What do we already know?

  • Current Operations: Founder-led development and deployment of MVPs; direct outreach to potential clients. Daily, for acquisition of customers, we identify potential customers, analyze their Business Strategy, IT/digital/AI strategy and draft plan to outreach. For product, we refine the existing chatbot technically and estimate the automation to accelerate the creation and deployment on Clickup and Github.
  • New operation at the end of 2025: We aim to find a co-founder and our daily operation will be primarily shared on Github integrated with internal AI agent for marketing, strategy, and routine process. We will have our own AI agent for processing and managing our marketing activities. We will apply Kanban/Scrum to track daily operation and iteration.
2

What do we want to know?

  • Optimal processes for onboarding new clients including demo efficiently and technically by UI/UX. Can we implement by ourselves or do we need other web design team(probably not). Automate process for creation and deployment of Demo.
  • Requirements for scaling operations, including potential hires and infrastructure needs.
  • Hiring engineer: if possible, we will not hire engineer but maximize AI agent. If we can acquire subscribed customer (ideally 3-5), we may consider hiring a dedicated engineer to maintain technical tasks. In that case,2 founders will be focusing on business development.
  • Our own AI Agent: Possibility of our own AI agent for Marketing, Strategy, Finance before the end of 2025 to compensate our shortage of team members. How long can we sustain without hiring other than co-founder.
  • Operation platform: short-term before the end of 2026
3

What don’t we know?

  • Time and resources required for customizing solutions for different clients.
  • Potential challenges in maintaining service quality as the client base grows.
  • When to scale

- S -

  • Lean operations; flexibility in adapting to customer needs. Tailor MVP for demo.

- W -

  • Limited manpower; potential bottlenecks in scaling. Low speed in outreach.

- O -

  • Streamlining processes for rapid deployment; leveraging automation tools.

- T -

  • Operational strain with increasing client demands; reliance on cloud service providers. Potential financial concern on Google cloud.

Takeaway:

  • Develop standardized UI for create chatbot with one click and deployment procedures.
  • Github for future operation visualization and management
