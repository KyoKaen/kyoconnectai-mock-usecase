Business Plan Version 0.0

KyoConnectAI Startup:

Section: 1. Problem

1

What do we already know?

  • SMBs and mid-sized organizations (e.g., business schools, event organizers) receive high volumes of repetitive customer inquiries (FAQs), especially during peak seasons.
  • They often lack IT infrastructure, technical support, or AI knowledge to automate responses.
  • This results in delayed responses, inefficiency, low customer satisfaction, and increased operational pressure.
  • We validated this problem through:
    • Interviews and demos with SMBs, such as camping site, event organizers.
    • Feedback from EMLyon business school.
    • Conversations with event organizers (e.g., TopWomenTech Brussels).
2

What do we want to know?

  • To what extent this problem impacts organizations in different sectors (hospitality, education, events)?
  • Which additional pain points exist (multilingual support, CRM integration, personalization, customer lead, customer trend, curiosity, customer data insight) apart from FQAs?
3

What don’t we know?

  • How different sectors would prioritize chatbot integration. For example, camping sites will prioritize during peak season. Event organizer expects before and during event. Business School may want to focus on application season.
  • The exact opportunity cost per business due to these inefficiencies. How much a camping site pay for receptionist to address customer FQAs. How much cost them using 3rd part outsource the customer calls.
  • How much would they be willing to pay for based on the customer volume. How much queries they receive average during peak season.

- S -

  • Problem validated across sectors; broad need; consistent feedback.

- W -

  • Missing quantified data on performance losses.

- O -

  • Expand to museums, travel agencies, and educational institutions.

- T -
  • Market perception of chatbots as impersonal or low-quality may hinder adoption.

Takeaway:

  • • Gather deeper metrics to quantify time saved and customer satisfaction boost.
  • • Test vertical-specific use cases and value propositions.
