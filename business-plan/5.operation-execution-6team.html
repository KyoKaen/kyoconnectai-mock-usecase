Business Plan Version 0.0

KyoConnectAI Startup:

Section: 5. Operations & Executions & 6. Team

1

What do we already know?

  • Current Operations: Founder-led development and deployment of MVPs; direct outreach to potential clients. Daily activities include identifying potential customers, analyzing their business/IT/digital/AI strategy, and drafting outreach plans. Product refinement occurs on the existing chatbot technically, with estimations of automation to accelerate creation and deployment tracked on Clickup and Github.
  • New Operation (End of 2025): Aim to find a co-founder. Daily operations will be primarily shared on Github, integrated with an internal AI agent for marketing, strategy, and routine process management. Kanban/Scrum will be applied to track daily operation and iteration.
  • Current Team: Solo founder with over 8 years of experience in the IT industry.
  • Co-founder Profile: Expertise in business development, fluent in French, and passionate about AI solutions for SMBs.
  • Objective: Achieve 0-3 customer acquisitions before the end of 2025.
2

What do we want to know?

  • Optimal processes for onboarding new clients, including efficient and technically sound demo processes by UI/UX. Is internal implementation feasible, or is external web design team needed? Can we automate the process for demo creation and deployment?
  • Requirements for scaling operations, including potential hires and infrastructure needs.
  • Hiring Engineer: Is maximizing AI agent sufficient to avoid hiring engineers? If subscription customer acquisition is successful (3-5 ideally), can a dedicated engineer maintain technical tasks while founders focus on business development?
  • Our own AI Agent: Feasibility of developing our own AI Agent for Marketing, Strategy, and Finance before the end of 2025 to compensate for team shortages. How long can the company sustain itself without hires beyond a co-founder?
  • Operation platform: Short-term plan before the end of 2026.
  • When is the right time to build a team? Is the plan to wait until the end of 2025, after real paid customer acquisition, before building a team?
  • Potential candidates for the co-founder role, their alignment with the company's vision and values, and best practices for equity distribution among early team members.
  • Optimal team structure for the next 12-24 months. Is an engineer truly necessary after automating most processes?
  • Can we leverage AI agents to cover Strategy, Marketing, and Finance responsibilities?
