KyoConnectAI – Executive Summary

AI Agent Platform for Repetitive Tasks, Starting with Customer Service Automation

Problem

SMBs across industries waste thousands of euros monthly on repetitive tasks like FAQs (e.g., campsite bookings, rental inquiries, product questions). Manual responses are slow, costly, and scale poorly during peak demand (validated via interviews with 2 campsites and [X] other SMBs).

Our Solution

A customizable AI agent platform that evolves with client needs:

  • Phase 1 (Now): 24/7 multilingual FAQ chatbot (90% accuracy in production).
  • Phase 2 (2025): Task automation (e.g., bookings, rental applications).
  • Phase 3 (2026+): Full-service agent (CRM sync, dynamic responses).

Why Now?

  • Cross-industry demand: Campsites, rentals, e-commerce...etc all face identical pain points.
  • No-code AI adoption: SMBs now embrace tools like ours—no IT skills needed.
  • Architectural edge: We design for horizontal scalability (one platform, multiple industries).

Traction

  • Live MVP: Deployed at a Camping Site with 90% query resolution accuracy.
  • Pipeline: In discussions with other campsites.

Business Model

  • Subscription SaaS: €100–€300/month (tiered by features).
  • High margins: 85%+ gross margin (cloud costs <€15/user at scale).
  • Horizontal scaling: Same core tech serves rentals, e-commerce, hospitality.

Team

  • Technical Founder: 8+ years in AI, Cloud, tech, IT architect of current MVP.
  • Co-Founder: Ex-[Company], 8+ years in GTM strategy for SMB SaaS.
  • Hiring Plan: First engineer at 5 paying clients (target mid-2025).

Vision

To automate repetitive tasks for any SMB—starting with conversations, expanding to end-to-end operations.
